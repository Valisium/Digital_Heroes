Using Copilot safely

Safe & secure

Copilot is our approved AI assistant for work. Do not put Whitbread information into other public AI tools such as ChatGPT or Gemini.

How it protects you

  • Work mode keeps your content protected.
  • Web mode searches the public web.
  • Copilot sees only what you can already see.

Using Copilot wisely

  • Double-check answers before you rely on them.
  • Treat answers as confidentially as the source files.
  • Older files from past projects may surface.
  • If you are unsure, ask the Digital Heroes team.
Did you know? Around 1 in 3 pieces of work data leaving organisations now flows through unsanctioned AI tools. Copilot is our approved assistant, so everything you do stays safe, secure and inside Whitbread.

Prompts to try

Try these first

Copy a prompt, paste it into Copilot, then make it yours. Filter by the app you want to start with.

Recap a meeting

Let Copilot keep track of key topics and action items so you can stay focused during the meeting and avoid listening to the recording after.

Draft an email with notes and action items from the meeting.

Summarise an email thread

Get quickly caught up on a long, complex email thread without scrolling through every reply.

Summarise this email thread and list any actions for me.

Draft an email

Personalise the tone and length so your message lands exactly how you intend.

Draft an email to [name] telling them Project X is delayed two weeks. Keep it casual in tone.

Summarise a document

Get right down to business by summarising long documents and focusing on the relevant sections.

Looking ahead

Agents in Copilot

Agents are Copilot helpers built for a specific job. Think of them as focused teammates: you give one a task, it works through the steps using trusted Microsoft 365 content, and hands you back a result. They run inside the apps you already use, within your existing Whitbread permissions.

🔒

What you have access to. To start, you will be able to use the ready-made Microsoft-built agents below.

Your starter agents

🤝

Facilitator

Keeps meetings on track. It captures notes, decisions and action items in real time so everyone leaves clear on what happens next.

🔍

Researcher

Digs into a question for you. It pulls together information from your work content and trusted sources into a clear, sourced summary.

📊

Analyst

Works through data for you. It spots patterns, builds simple breakdowns and explains what the numbers are telling you in plain English.
