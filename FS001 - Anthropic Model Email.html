First, what is an AI model?

Before we get to the update, here is the whole idea in plain English.

An AI model is like a very well read assistant. It has learned from a huge amount of text how words and ideas fit together, so it can put together a sensible answer in seconds.

The proper name for this is a large language model, or LLM. Claude and GPT are two different ones. Each is better at certain jobs, so having more than one to choose from means better results for you.

THE UPDATE

What is changing

Microsoft 365 Copilot now has more than one AI engine under the bonnet. Alongside the OpenAI GPT models you already use, Claude from Anthropic is fully enabled across the Whitbread tenant and ready for you to use.

Not a new product

Copilot remains your single AI tool. Claude is an additional model option, so you never leave the Copilot interface.

More capability

Claude is strong at deep reasoning, nuanced writing, and complex multi-step tasks. More choice means better results.

Same security

Claude operates within the Microsoft enterprise environment. Your data stays protected and nothing changes.

SEE IT IN ACTION

Same question, different answers

Ask the same question to a different model and you will often get the same accurate answer, just presented in a different way. It is a bit like asking two people in a room the same thing. Both will most likely give you the right answer, depending on what you ask of course, but each one explains it in their own style.

Give it a go yourself

Open Microsoft 365 Copilot on the web in two browser windows, side by side. Pick a different model in each one, ask them both the same question, and see what comes back. It is the quickest way to find the style that suits you.

New Researcher features unlocked

Researcher, the deep research agent in Copilot, now puts Claude and GPT to work together on the same task. There are two ways to use them.

Critique

One model plans the research and writes the first draft. A second model then reviews it for accuracy, completeness, and source quality before the report reaches you. This is the default, so simply choose Auto in the Researcher model picker.

Council

Claude and GPT each produce a full report on your question at the same time. A separate model then compares them and shows where they agree, where they differ, and what each one adds. Choose Model Council in the picker when a question calls for extra confidence.

Microsoft reports a 13.8% improvement on its deep research quality benchmark when the models work together. Read the Microsoft announcement
