Welcome

Following Mark’s announcement, we wanted to introduce ourselves properly. If you missed it, you can read his note again on the Digital Heroes Hub. We are Harprit Takkar and Chris Adams, and together we lead Digital Heroes, the programme supporting the rollout of Microsoft 365 Copilot across Whitbread.

We ran a pilot earlier this year and the response was overwhelmingly positive, so the decision was made to roll Copilot out to everyone. You may already have heard from colleagues in the pilot about the difference it has made to their working day. Now it is your turn to experience it.

 

Over the coming weeks we will help you become confident with Copilot in your own right, knowing how to put it to work and recover real time in your day. You do not need to be technical. A little curiosity is all that is required, and we will support you with the rest.

How the programme works

We will run the sessions, share the prompts and keep the programme moving. The rest is down to you. The more colleagues who take part with real work and real questions, the more valuable the programme becomes for everyone.

What your licence unlocks

Until now, you may have used Copilot Chat for quick questions and answers. Your new Copilot Premium licence goes considerably further. It works directly inside the applications you use every day, drawing on your own documents, emails and files so it can draft, summarise and bring information together in a fraction of the time.

 
Coming in your training

Your licence also unlocks Work IQ, one of the features we will introduce during your training. We will not cover it in detail here, but it is well worth looking forward to.

What to expect

Your licence will be switched on on Monday 29th June. Shortly after, you will receive a short Welcome Guide from us, including a quick start guide, a set of practical tips and some content you can begin using straight away.
