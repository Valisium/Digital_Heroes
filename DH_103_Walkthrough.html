Teams and meetings

Copilot live in a meeting

Teams, in a meeting
“What did this person say about that topic?”
“Start drafting the action I have just been given and give me a baseline to work from.”
Best practice

Works when the call is transcribed. Stay present in the room and let Copilot catch the detail.

Facilitator

Facilitator
“Facilitator, add an action for me to catch up with this person next week.”
Best practice

A ready-made agent for meetings. Let it handle notes and actions into Planner, so you can give the room your attention.

Researcher and Analyst

Deep research takes a little while, so use a prepared example.

Researcher, Critique

Researcher
“Research this topic and give me the key findings with sources.”
Best practice

Auto runs Critique by default: one model researches, a second reviews sources and citations before you see it.

Researcher, Model Council

Researcher
“Research this topic, and use Model Council so I can compare how the models see it.”
Best practice

Runs more than one model at once. Reach for it on the bigger or more contested questions.

Analyst

Analyst
“Analyse this data and tell me the main trends and any outliers.”
Best practice

Reach for an agent on the bigger questions. Your expertise stays in charge of the fine detail.
