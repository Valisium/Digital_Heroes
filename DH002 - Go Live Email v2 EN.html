Go-Live

This is the moment! Your Premium Microsoft 365 Copilot licence is switched on and ready to go. From today, Copilot works right alongside you inside the apps you already use every day. Welcome aboard. We are so glad to have you with us!

What is different now

Up to now you may have used Copilot Chat for quick questions. The Premium licence goes further. Copilot now works inside Word, Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint and Teams, and it can use your own files and emails to help you draft, summarise and find what you need in less time.

Three steps to get started

1
Read the welcome guide. It is linked below and takes you through your first steps with Copilot.
2
Sign in. Open any Microsoft 365 app, find the Copilot button, and sign in with your Whitbread account.
3
Try one thing today. Ask Copilot to summarise a long email thread or draft a reply. A small start is all it takes.
